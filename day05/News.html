Tirumala is a Hindu religious temple town in 
Tirupati district of the Indian state of Andhra Pradesh.
It is one of the neighbourhoods of the Tirupati city. 
The town is a part of Tirupati Urban Development Authority and
located in Tirupati (urban) mandal of Tirupati revenue division.
The town is strictly vegetarian. 
It is a hill town where Tirumala Venkateswara Temple is located, 
a popular shrine of Vishnu. Vishnu is believed to 
reside here with his full power, as in Vaikuntha, 
and thus the place is also called 'Bhuloka Vaikuntha'.
