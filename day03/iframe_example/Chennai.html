Welcome to Chennai

Chennai, formerly known as Madras, is known for its rich cultural heritage, vibrant arts scene, and significant historical landmarks. It's a major hub for classical dance and music, particularly Bharatanatyam and Carnatic music, and is also a center for Tamil cinema. Additionally, Chennai is a major industrial and technological center, often called the "Detroit of South Asia" due to its automobile industry, and is home to numerous IT parks.
